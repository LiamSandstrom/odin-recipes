Healthy oat bowl

Description

Quick, easy, and healthy. This is perfect as a breakfast or snack!

Ingredients

  • Oats : 40g
  • Protein powder : 1 scoop
  • Milk : 1dl
  • Yoghurt : 1dl
  • Frozen berries : 30g

Steps

  1. Add oats, protein powder, milk and the frozen berries to a bowl
  2. Microwave for 1 minute
  3. Top with yoghurt
