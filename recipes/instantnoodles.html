Faster noodles than Mr.Allen!

Description

Quick, easy, and Tasty. The snack of an adult, the meal of the student!

Ingredients

  • Samyang Ramen

Steps

  1. Add the noodles to a saucepan
  2. fill saucepan with water until it covers the noodles
  3. Put on stove from 6 minutes
  4. Drain half of the leftover water and add to a bowl
  5. Add 3/4 of the flavouring packet to the bowl and mix
