Tender Tastyyy tacos today!

Description

Are you ready for a quick, easy, tasty and healthy meal? this recipe takes just 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Minced chicken : 800g
  • Tortilla bread: 400g
  • Black beans : 400g
  • Kale : 100g
  • Salsa : 200g
  • Sour cream : 2dl
  • Honey : 1 table spoon
  • Garlic spice : 1 tea spoon
  • Red pepper spice : 1 table spoon
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the pan to 7/9 heat
  2. Add the minced chicken
  3. Cook until almost all chicken is white
  4. Add the spices and honey to the pan
  5. Strain the black beans and add them to the pan
  6. Cook for 5 minutes
  7. Microwave the Tortilla for 20 seconds
  8. Cut up the kale in tiny pieces
  9. Serve the kale and chicken on the Tortillas with salsa and sour cream!
